How to play Quake II (1997) in 2023

Quake II is a 1997 first-person-shooter developed by id Software. This guide will show you how to play the game on modern PCs.

To play Quake II in this day and age, you can purchase it through Steam, GOG.com or the Epic Games Store. For this guide, we'll be using Steam.

  • Download and install the Steam app through its offical website.
  • Create an account and sign in if you don't already have one.
  • In the "Store" section of the app, type "quake" in the search bar and Quake II should appear in the results. Clicking on it should take you to the game's Steam page.
  • Click on the "ADD TO CART" button below the game description.
  • On the shopping cart page, click the "Purchase for myself" option.
  • You should be taken to a screen where you can choose the option of purchasing via PayPal or your credit card. Either option will suffice.
  • After finalizing your purchase, the game should now show up in your Steam Libray. Install the game and click the "PLAY" button to start it up.
